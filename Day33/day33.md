# Day 33 – Media Integrity Analyzer

## 🚀 Overview

For Day 33 of the **60 Days of Claude AI Challenge by ABTalksOnAI**, I built a **Media Integrity Analyzer** using Claude AI.

This interactive application helps users understand how headlines, articles, and social media posts can influence perception through sensational language, emotional manipulation, and audience targeting.

Instead of simply explaining media literacy concepts, the project guides users through realistic scenarios where they evaluate content first and then receive a detailed analysis of the techniques used.

---

## 🎯 Project Features

- 🎨 Multiple visual themes
- 📰 Headline Detective Challenge
- 💭 Emotion Detection Challenge
- 📊 Live Media Integrity Metrics
- 🎯 Randomized scenarios for every session
- 🔍 Headline vs. article comparison
- ⚠️ Identification of misleading or exaggerated claims
- ❤️ Emotional manipulation analysis
- 👥 Audience targeting insights
- ✍️ Neutral headline and content rewrites
- 📈 Personal Media Integrity Dashboard
- 🔄 Replay with new scenarios

---

## 🧠 Key Learnings

### 1. Headlines don't always tell the full story

One of the biggest lessons from this project was understanding how headlines can exaggerate or distort information to attract attention, even when the actual article presents a much more balanced perspective.

---

### 2. Emotional language influences decision-making

Content creators often use words that trigger fear, anger, urgency, or excitement to encourage clicks and shares. Recognizing these patterns makes it easier to separate emotional reactions from factual information.

---

### 3. Audience targeting shapes content

Many posts are designed for specific audiences by appealing to their concerns, beliefs, or aspirations. Identifying the intended audience helps explain why certain messages feel especially persuasive.

---

### 4. Interactive learning improves understanding

Rather than presenting definitions, allowing users to analyze headlines and social media posts before revealing the explanation creates a more engaging and memorable learning experience.

---

### 5. AI accelerates educational application development

Claude AI helped generate UI components, application flow, educational content, and interactive logic, allowing me to focus on designing an engaging learning experience instead of starting everything from scratch.
