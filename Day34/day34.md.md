# Day 44 – Marketing Detective

## 🚀 Overview

For Day 44 of the **60 Days of Claude AI Challenge by ABTalksOnAI**, I built **Marketing Detective**, an interactive case-solving application using Claude AI.

In this project, users take on the role of a marketing detective who investigates real-world marketing campaign failures. By analyzing campaign briefs, customer feedback, performance metrics, and social media insights, users identify the primary marketing mistake before reviewing a detailed explanation and improvement recommendations.

Instead of simply reading marketing theory, users actively investigate evidence and learn through hands-on problem solving.

---

## 🎯 Project Features

- 🕵️ Interactive marketing investigation game
- 📂 Randomly generated marketing case studies
- 📋 Campaign briefing and business objectives
- 📌 Draggable evidence board for investigation
- 📊 Campaign performance metrics
- 💬 Customer comments and feedback analysis
- 📱 Social media performance insights
- 🧩 Multiple-choice root cause investigation
- 📈 Detective accuracy scoring
- 📖 Detailed learning report
- 💡 Actionable marketing improvement recommendations
- 🎨 Multiple visual themes
- 🔄 Replay with new cases

---

## 🧠 Key Learnings

### 1. Marketing is about solving business problems

Successful marketing isn't just about increasing reach or engagement. Every campaign must support business goals, customer experience, and conversions.

---

### 2. Data tells a story

Campaign metrics alone don't reveal the full picture. Combining customer feedback, social media conversations, and business results provides a much clearer understanding of what actually happened.

---

### 3. Root cause analysis is essential

Many marketing failures have multiple symptoms, but identifying the true underlying issue is what leads to effective solutions.

---

### 4. Interactive learning improves retention

Investigating evidence before revealing the correct answer makes learning more engaging and helps reinforce marketing concepts through practical application.

---

### 5. AI accelerates educational product development

Claude AI helped generate application structure, case scenarios, interface components, and interactive logic, allowing me to focus on designing an engaging learning experience.
