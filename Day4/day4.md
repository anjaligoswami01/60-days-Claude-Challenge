# 🚀 Day 4 – Chain-of-Thought Prompting

## 📌 Overview

This project is part of the **Claude 60 Days Challenge** organized by **ABTalks**.

The goal of Day 4 was to learn and apply **Chain-of-Thought (CoT) Prompting**, a prompting technique that encourages AI models to reason through a problem step-by-step before generating a final response.

---

## 🎯 Objective

* Learn the fundamentals of Chain-of-Thought Prompting.
* Use Claude AI to generate a personalized career roadmap.
* Understand how structured reasoning improves AI outputs.
* Document learnings, insights, and observations.

---

## 🛠️ Steps Performed

1. Read the provided resources on Chain-of-Thought Prompting.
2. Opened a new conversation in Claude.
3. Used the provided CoT prompt template.
4. Answered four career-related questions.
5. Generated a personalized career roadmap.
6. Reviewed the roadmap and identified key insights.
7. Documented findings in GitHub.

---

## 🧠 Key Learnings

* Chain-of-Thought prompting improves response quality by encouraging step-by-step reasoning.
* Providing detailed context helps AI generate more personalized recommendations.
* Structured prompts produce clearer and more actionable outputs.
* AI can be effectively used for career planning and goal setting.

---

## 💡 Biggest Insight

The roadmap highlighted the importance of consistently building real-world projects and documenting them publicly on GitHub to strengthen an AI Engineer portfolio and improve career opportunities.
