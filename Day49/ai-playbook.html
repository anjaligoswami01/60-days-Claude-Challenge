Loop Builder

Turn any one-shot prompt into an autonomous loop: instructions telling the AI to check its own work against your criteria, improve it, and know when to stop.

↻

What is a "loop"? A normal prompt asks the AI for one answer. A loop prompt instructs the AI to draft, self-critique against rules you set, revise, and repeat — until it meets your bar or hits a safe stopping point. Useful for polishing a proposal draft, tightening an email's tone, or iterating on marketing copy without you reviewing every pass.

The base instruction you want turned into a loop — e.g. "Write a follow-up email to a client who went quiet."
What "done well" looks like for this task.
How the AI should judge each draft before revising.
How the AI should revise between passes.
When the AI should stop looping and hand back the result.
Guardrails so the loop doesn't drift — facts, claims, or tone it must never change.
