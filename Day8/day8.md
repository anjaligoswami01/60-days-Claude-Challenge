# 🌍 Personal Environmental Health Analyzer

**Day 8/60 – Claude AI Challenge by ABTalksOnAI**

A responsive AI-powered dashboard that analyzes **Air Quality Index (AQI)** and **Water Quality** data to provide environmental health insights, risk assessments, and personalized recommendations for healthier living.

## ✨ Features

* Real-time AQI and Water Quality analysis
* Environmental Health Score
* Interactive visualizations and dashboards
* Health impact assessment
* City comparison insights
* Personalized recommendations for air, water, skin, hair, and outdoor activities
* Mobile-friendly dark theme UI

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Environmental data becomes more valuable when converted into actionable health recommendations.
* Data visualization helps users quickly understand environmental risks.
* Combining AQI and water quality provides a more complete picture of personal environmental health.

## 📚 Key Learnings

* Building user-centric dashboards for complex datasets.
* Data cleaning, validation, and handling missing values.
* Designing responsive and interactive visualizations.
* Using Claude AI to rapidly prototype real-world applications.
* Transforming raw environmental data into meaningful insights.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

* HTML, CSS, JavaScript
* Environmental Data APIs
* Claude AI
